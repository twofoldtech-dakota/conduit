# Conduit Pricing (One-Pager)

Primary model: Annual self-hosted license. Value metric: production sites/projects + adapters + X-Ray scans.

## Packages
- Developer (non-prod): Free
  - 1 non-prod site, 1 adapter, 25 scans/mo, community support
- Business: $4,000/site/year
  - 2 adapters, 200k items/mo, 100 X-Ray scans/mo, Standard support (8x5, 2-biz-day SLA)
- Growth (up to 5 sites): $12,000/year
  - 4 adapters, 1M items/mo, 500 scans/mo, Priority support (8x5, 4-hour first response)
- Enterprise (org-wide): from $30,000/year
  - Unlimited sites, unlimited adapters, 3M items/mo, 2,000 scans/mo, 24x7 P1 (1-hour), security & procurement support

## Add-ons & Overage
- Extra adapter: $1,500/year
- Extra scans: $1/scan (monthly arrears)
- Extra throughput: $500 per additional 1M items/month
- Professional services: $250/hr; Custom adapter from $8,000
- POC: $6,000 for 2 weeks (credited on conversion)

## Discounts & Terms
- Annual prepay 15%; 2-year 20%
- Agency/partner 10% with 3+ client deployments
- Payment Net 30; auto-renew 12 months; price protection for current term

## SaaS Option (Future)
- Starter $199/mo (1 site, 1 adapter, 100 scans), Team $799/mo, Business $1,999/mo, Enterprise custom

## Order Form (3 line items)
1. Package (Business/Growth/Enterprise)
2. Adapters (quantity)
3. Services/POC
