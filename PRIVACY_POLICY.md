# Privacy Policy (Draft)

This Privacy Policy describes how Conduit (self-hosted) processes data.

Scope
This policy applies to the Software operated by you in your environment. Twofold Tech does not receive customer content processed by your self-hosted instance unless you choose to share it for support purposes.

Data processed by Conduit

- CMS content and metadata necessary to perform requested operations
- X-Ray scan results (if enabled)
- Operational logs (if enabled)

Your responsibilities

- Provide notice to and obtain any required consents from data subjects
- Configure retention and access controls according to your policies
- Avoid sending special categories of personal data unless necessary

Security

- Store credentials securely (e.g., Vault, AWS Secrets Manager)
- Restrict network access to the Software
- Review and apply security updates

Retention
You control retention within your environment. If X-Ray persistence is enabled, configure backup and retention periods.

International transfers
As a self-hosted product, transfers are controlled by your deployment choices.

Contact
For privacy inquiries, contact privacy@twofold.tech.

This is a non-binding draft. Consult counsel before using with customers.
